Email Regulations & Etiquette

  • ✔ Keep subject concise: The recipient should know what the email is about at a glance.
  • ✔ Be respectful: Always use formal salutations (Dear, Respected).
  • ✔ Mention your purpose early: Don't make the reader guess why you are writing.
  • ✔ Keep paragraphs short: Large blocks of text are hard to read.
  • ✔ End with gratitude: Always thank the recipient for their time.

Meeting Authority Figures

  • 👔 Dress professionally: First impressions matter.
  • 🚪 Knock before entering: Always wait for permission to enter their office.
  • 🙂 Introduce yourself politely: State your name and your department/class clearly.
  • 🗣 Mention you've emailed: "I am following up on the email I sent yesterday regarding..."
  • 🙏 Thank the authority: Appreciate them taking the time out of their busy schedule.
